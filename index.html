üéµ China Tour Invoice Generator - User Guide

What is this?

A simple web-based tool that generates professional invoices for the China Tour (Mozart & Elisabeth productions). No installation needed, works offline, completely free!

How to Use

Step 1: Open the File

  1. Find the file china-tour-invoice-generator.html
  2. Double-click it to open in your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge - any will work!)
  3. That's it! No internet connection needed.

Step 2: Fill in Your Information

The form has 4 sections:

üë§ Personal Information

  • Your full name
  • Your address (street, postal code, city, country)
  • Your email
  • Your tax number

üè¶ Bank Information

  • Bank name
  • IBAN
  • BIC

üìã Contract Information

  • Production Type: Choose one of:
    • Both Productions (Mozart + Elisabeth)
    • Mozart Only
    • Elisabeth Only
  • Total Contract Amount: The total amount in your contract (e.g., 15000)
  • Invoice Month: Which month you're invoicing for (November, December, January, or February)

üßæ Invoice Details

  • Invoice Number: Your sequential invoice number (e.g., 2025-09)
  • Invoice Date: The date appears automatically (today), but you can change it

Step 3: Generate Invoice

Click the big purple "Generate Invoice" button at the bottom!

Step 4: Save as PDF

  1. The invoice will appear below the form
  2. Click the green "Save as PDF / Print" button
  3. In the print dialog:
    • Select "Save as PDF" as your printer/destination
    • Click Save
  4. Done! Send this PDF to invoice@nordicview.dk (CC: liveirin@nordicview.dk)
